Oncologist Access

Oncologist access enables a user to look up data associated with individual patients. Patient-specific data includes results from molecular tests and genomically-driven clinical trial matches.

Oncologist access is automatically granted to all users with existing access to Results Viewer.

If you do not have results viewer access, you can apply by submitting a ticket to Partners IT through ServiceNow.

Clinical Trial Investigator Access

Clinical Trial Investigator access enables users involved in running a clinical trial to identify patients for their trials. Access may be granted to clinical trial investigators and support staff.

Clinical Trial Investigator access is controlled by the Office of Clinical Research (OCR).

To apply for access, please fill out the form below.
