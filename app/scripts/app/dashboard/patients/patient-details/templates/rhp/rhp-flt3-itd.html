*This assay has been validated to detect FLT3-ITDs with insert size up to 180 bp. AR (allelic ratio) is approximated as Mutant/Wildtype or Sum(Mutants)/Wildtype and is calculated from the variant allele fraction by the following formula: AR=FLT3-ITD alleles / wild-type alleles. However, the AR may be underestimated for certain ITDs by the technical limitations of the assay and should be considered semi-quantitative only for these cases.
