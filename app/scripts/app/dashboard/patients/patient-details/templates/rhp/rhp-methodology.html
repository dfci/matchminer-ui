Description

The BWH Rapid Heme Panel (RHP) Assay is a next generation sequencing assay based on the NEBNext kit from New England Biolabs, Inc. A detailed description of the assay is available upon request from Center for Advanced Molecular Diagnostics, Brigham and Women's Hospital.

Amplification method: NEBNext Direct(R), New England Biolabs, Inc.
Assay Version: Rapid Heme Panel V3.0
Genes: Total 88,(183 KB) whole coding sequence for most genes
Sequencing platform: Illumina NextSeq 550Dx, 150bp paired-end reads
Raw data processing: BWA Mem (v0.7.17)
UMI Correction: Fgbio (v0.4.0)
Variant Caller: Vardict (v1.6.0)
CNV Caller: RobustCNV (internally developed)
FLT3-ITD: TsaiITD (internally developed)
Data File formats: BAM, VCF
Genome version: hg19
Metrics: GATK (v.4.0.5.0) and Fgbio (v0.4.0)
Variant Annotation: Variant Effect Predictor (v79)
Pipeline cutoff: 3 Variant Reads and 1% variant allele frequency.
Analytical sensitivity: Varies from locus to locus, but is estimated to be 3.0% at 325x coverage

The test performed at Brigham and Women's Hospital were developed and their performance characteristics determined by the Molecular Diagnostic Laboratory in the Department of Pathology at BWH. They have not been cleared or approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA). The FDA has determined that such clearance or approval is not necessary.
