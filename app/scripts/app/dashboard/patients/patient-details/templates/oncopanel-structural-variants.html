Caution

Samples sequenced prior to December 2018 had structural variants reported as plain text, e.g. 'Patient is positive for EML4-ALK fusion...', as opposed to structured data, e.g. geneA=EML4, geneB=ALK.

For samples with this unstructured data, it is possible that MatchMiner may miss or incorrectly classify some structural variants. For example, a filter for ALK structural variants will match a patient who was 'Negative for ALK rearrangements'. Users should therefore always carefully check the structural variant comment for each sample before making a final decision.
